Privacy notice

Effective July 17, 2026

The Cold Report is a publication of Sargon Labs LLC. This notice explains the information we collect, why we use it, and the choices available to you.

Information we collect

We may collect information you submit, including your name, email address, company, role, interests, geography, product interest, correspondence, and subscription or billing details. Payment-card data is processed by our payment provider and is not stored by The Cold Report.

We may also receive basic technical and aggregate usage information such as page viewed, referring page, device type, approximate region, and timestamps. We do not sell personal information or use it to build third-party advertising profiles.

How we use information

We use information to deliver requested publications and services; operate watchlists and alerts; respond to questions and corrections; process and administer subscriptions; protect the service; understand aggregate readership; satisfy legal obligations; and improve our coverage and products.

Providers and disclosure

Service providers may process information on our behalf for publishing, email delivery, payments, hosting, analytics, and operational support. Current production providers will be named here when their connections are activated. We may disclose information when required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a corporate transaction. We do not permit providers to use subscriber information for their own advertising.

Cookies and analytics

The current public site does not intentionally use advertising cookies or behavioral-tracking pixels. We audit cookies and similar storage before enabling analytics or embedded services. If nonessential cookies are introduced, we will provide the notice and choice required for affected visitors.

Retention, security, and choices

We retain information only as reasonably necessary for the purposes above, legal obligations, dispute resolution, and suppression of addresses that have opted out. No transmission or storage system is perfectly secure.

You may unsubscribe using the link in a publication. You may also request access, correction, deletion, or information about our handling of your data by emailing privacy@thecoldreport.com. Rights vary by location, and we may need to verify a request before acting.

International visitors and changes

The service is operated from the United States. Information may be processed in the United States and other countries where our providers operate. We will revise this notice when payment, newsletter, analytics, or other material processing changes. The effective date above identifies the current version.

Privacy questions may be sent to privacy@thecoldreport.com.
