About The Cold Report

The Cold Report is an early-warning intelligence publication for the companies building and selling into temperature-controlled infrastructure. It covers pharmaceuticals, food, ocean reefer, road freight, cold storage, air cargo, investment, and operating capacity.

The job

The market produces more information than insight: permits without purchasing context, announcements without timing, capital plans without likely packages, and news that stops just before the commercial question. We connect those fragments to the decision a vendor, operator, or investor may need to make next.

The free weekday brief builds the shared market view. The Project Radar follows expansions, new facilities, acquisitions, route launches, tenders, capacity changes, and distress events as living records—identifying likely needs, buyer functions, timing, and the next trigger worth watching.

The difference

We aim to find meaningful change earlier, translate it into a commercial consequence, make the possible buyer and need specific, and keep watching until the signal advances or dies. Facts, interpretation, and unknowns remain visibly separate.

The publication uses AI-assisted research and production with human-defined standards, source requirements, validation, and release controls. AI is part of the operating system; evidence and useful judgment are the product.
