About The Cold Report

The Cold Report is an independent intelligence publication for the infrastructure, companies, and people that keep temperature-sensitive goods moving. It covers pharmaceuticals, food, ocean reefer, road freight, cold storage, air cargo, investment, and operating capacity.

The weekday report turns a noisy market into a concise operating read. The Opportunity Radar extends that work into structured records of projects, expansions, acquisitions, facilities, route launches, tenders, and distress events.

The publication uses AI-assisted research and production with human-defined standards, source requirements, and release controls. AI is part of the operating model, not a substitute for evidence.
